From a Small Studio to Global Reach

Our journey began in a dusty basement in East London, where three writers shared a single dream: to give a platform to the stories that the world was too busy to hear. What started as a small independent press has grown into a global beacon for literary excellence.

In 2012, we introduced the Gold Label Series, a collection dedicated to reviving forgotten classics with modern academic commentary and bespoke artistic covers. This initiative redefined our brand, marrying the wisdom of the past with the design sensibilities of the future.

Today, Duck Store is proud to represent authors from over 40 countries, bridging cultural divides and fostering a more empathetic world through the shared language of literature.
