From a Small Studio to Global Reach

Our journey began in the back corner of the faculty library at FCAI, where three students shared a single dream: to digitize the knowledge that was trapped in decaying bindings and outdated catalogs. What started as a script to automate book tracking has grown into a sophisticated ecosystem for global information retrieval.

In 2024, we launched the Deep-Archive Series, a project dedicated to migrating ancient manuscripts into cloud-native databases with modern OCR commentary and bespoke metadata structures. This initiative redefined our mission, marrying the timeless wisdom of the library stacks with the high-speed data structures of the future.

Today, we are proud to connect readers and researchers from over 40 countries, bridging the gap between physical archives and digital accessibility, fostering a more informed world through the shared language of Data and Literature.
